2026 Kyoto Early Summer Trip

May 14 – 18

01 DAY · May 14
🏯 Shijo & Eastern Kyoto
  • 10:00 → Check-in & drop bag at Shijo capsule hotel
  • 10:30 → Yasaka Shrine (quiet morning visit, skip crowds)
  • 11:30 → Maruyama Park → walk to Chion-in (massive wooden gate, quiet gardens)
  • 13:00 → Lunch at Shoren-in area (matcha soba or yudofu)
  • 14:00 → Shoren-in Temple → Konchi-in → Nanzen-ji (rock garden)
  • 16:00 → Eikan-do Temple → Philosopher's Path (golden hour walk)
  • Evening → Return to Shijo → night walk in Gion & Shirakawa River
02 DAY · May 15
🌸 Southern Higashiyama & Traditional Kyoto
  • 07:30 → Early start to Kiyomizu-dera (beat crowds)
  • 09:30 → Sannenzaka & Ninenzaka (stroll, pottery shops)
  • 11:00 → Kodai-ji Temple (zen gardens & bamboo grove)
  • 12:30 → Lunch in Gion area (kaiseki or sushi lunch set)
  • 14:00 → Kyoto Gyokuen (traditional tea ceremony experience)
  • 16:00 → Shimogamo Shrine → Kamigamo Shrine (ancient architecture)
  • Evening → Walk back to Shijo along Kamogawa River (riverbank sunset)
03 DAY · May 16
🚂 Uji & Nara Day Trip
  • 08:00 → Train to Uji (20 mins from Kyoto Station)
  • 09:00 → Byodo-in Temple (Phoenix Hall, UNESCO)
  • 10:30 → Ujigami Shrine (oldest Shinto shrine in Japan)
  • 11:30 → Matcha lunch & tea tasting at Nakamura Tokichi
  • 13:00 → Train to Nara (45 mins)
  • 14:00 → Todai-ji Temple (giant Buddha statue) → Nara Park (deer feeding)
  • 16:00 → Kasuga Taisha (thousands of stone lanterns)
  • 17:30 → Train back to Kyoto
  • Evening → Optional night stroll in Nishiki Market (street food)
04 DAY · May 17
🎋 Arashiyama & Western Kyoto
  • 08:30 → Train to Arashiyama (15 mins from Kyoto Station)
  • 09:30 → Otagi Nenbutsu-ji Temple (1200 stone statues, quiet hillside)
  • 11:00 → Bamboo Grove (early visit, minimal crowds) → Tenryu-ji Temple
  • 12:30 → Lunch in Arashiyama (tempura or yudofu)
  • 14:00 → Nonomiya Shrine (purification ritual) → Togetsukyo Bridge
  • 16:00 → Optional: Iwatayama Monkey Park or Arashiyama Monkey Park
  • 17:30 → Train back to Kyoto
  • Evening → Relax at Kyoto Station → rooftop sunset view
05 DAY · May 18
✈️ Final Morning & Departure
  • 07:30 → Morning run/walk along Kamogawa River
  • 09:00 → Brunch in Nishiki Market area (tamagoyaki, sushi rolls)
  • 10:30 → Pack & check out of hotel
  • 11:30 → Bus from Shijo to Kansai International Airport (1hr)
  • 14:20 → Departure flight back to Tokyo
※ A trip is not over when the wheels touch down. It is over when every receipt has either become a refund or become an accepted loss. The honest window for the first half of that work is the first two weeks. The rest is reminiscence.
