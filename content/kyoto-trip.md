# 2026 KYOTO EARLY SUMMER TRIP
May 14 – 18

---

## 01 DAY · May 14
Check-in & drop bag at Shijo capsule. 10:30 departure.

Walk sequence: Yasaka Shrine → Maruyama Park → Chion-in → Shoren-in → Konchi-in → Nanzen-ji → Eikan-do → Philosopher’s Path.

Evening: Return to Shijo, night walk around Gion & Shirakawa.

---

## 02 DAY · May 15
07:30 early start to Kiyomizu-dera → Sannenzaka → Ninenzaka → Kodai-ji.

Morning: Kyoto Gyokuen, watch Aoi Matsuri departure.

Afternoon: Shimogamo Shrine → Kamigamo Shrine, view Aoi Matsuri procession.

Evening: Walk back to Shijo along Kamogawa River.

---

## 03 DAY · May 16
Morning: Uji – Byodo-in, Ujigami Shrine, matcha.

Afternoon: Nara – Todai-ji, Nara Park, Kasuga Taisha.

Evening: Return to Kyoto. Optional night stroll in Nara.

---

## 04 DAY · May 17
09:00 arrive at Otagi Nenbutsu-ji, hike downhill through temples → Bamboo Grove → Nonomiya Shrine → Togetsukyo Bridge.

Afternoon: Explore Arashiyama freely.

Evening: Return to Kyoto, night walk at Kyoto Station.

---

## 05 DAY · May 18
Morning: Run along Kamogawa River, light stroll in Shijo / Nishiki Market.

12:20 departure from Shijo → Kyoto Station → airport bus to Itami.

Afternoon: Fly to Haneda, meet friends in Tokyo.
